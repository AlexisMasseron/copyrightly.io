opyrightLY

A Decentralized Application (√êApp) for Copyright Management based on the Ethereum blockchain.

Features

  • Manifestations: expressions of authors ideas into pieces of content that can be then used to prove authorship.
    • Register: claim single authorship on a piece of content hash.
    • Search: check if a piece of content is already registered searching by hash.
    • List Own: look for all the authorship manifestations made by the current user.
    • List All: look for all the authorship manifestations registered by any user.
    • Details: From a manifestation search results, or from the list of own manifestations, click "Details" to see all the information about them, including their Evidences.
    • Evidences: From the manifestation details it is possible to add new evidences supporting authorship.
  • More coming...
